Andrew Chang

Researcher in special education, language and literacy development, and evidence-based instructional practice.

My work focuses on understanding how language, literacy, and instructional supports shape academic outcomes for students with disabilities and students who need additional support in school-based settings.

Research

Language and Literacy Development

I study early language and literacy skills as foundations for later academic achievement, with particular attention to reading development and school-based assessment.

Reading Intervention

My work examines reading interventions, implementation, and instructional supports for students with persistent academic difficulties.

Research Synthesis

I use meta-analysis and systematic review methods to synthesize evidence and connect research findings to educational practice.
